  • Click 'New Game' and a random spooky secret answer will be chosen
  • Type any valid 5 letter word & press Enter to submit your guess
  • You have 6 chances to guess the secret word
  • Green = letter is in the correct spot
  • Yellow = correct letter in the wrong spot
  • Gray = letter is not included in the answer
